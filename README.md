### what this is
 
The Quantum CEO is the publishing home of *Quantum Currency: Field Research on Human Coherence* — the formal statement of thirteen years of field research into the architecture of coherent human systems.
 
The Quantum CEO is not a title or a job description. It's a state of being: the version of yourself that holds self-awareness and awareness of others simultaneously. Present, clear, grounded in your own system. The observer of time rather than the subject of it.

**Node 8 · structure · octagon** — quantum superposition. The observer capacity. Eight simultaneous orientations held in stable relationship without collapsing any of them prematurely. The prefrontal cortex arriving at the eighty-eighth day before birth — the observer always present, always watching, waiting for the noise floor to drop sufficiently that the pattern becomes legible.
