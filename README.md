## what this is

The Quantum CEO is a books and writing site — the formal publishing home of thirteen years of field research into the architecture of coherent human systems.

The Quantum CEO is not a title or a job description. It's a state of being: the version of yourself that holds self-awareness and awareness of others simultaneously. Present, clear, grounded in your own system. The observer of time rather than the subject of it.

This site houses:

- **The Book of Shadows series** — five intention-setting novellas (2016–ongoing), each with eight chapters, eight poems, and eight songs. Written for the busy professional, parent, and technologist who thinks in systems and feels in metaphor.
- **Quantum Currency: Field Research on Human Coherence** — the non-fiction companion. A rigorously researched, multi-disciplinary approach to the practical architecture of a coherent life that actually sustains you, the planet, and the future of tech.

## quantum currency

## design system

### node
**Node 8 · structure · octagon** — the observer capacity. Eight simultaneous orientations held in stable relationship. The geometry of the system that can sustain the full complexity of what is present without collapsing prematurely.

### accent colour
`#3c3489` — twilight indigo — 430nm on the visible spectrum — the colour of deep dusk, the moment before full dark, the last light before the field becomes the source.

### accent-lt
`#6a6ab5` — for hover states and pill nav backgrounds.

### CSS
```css
--accent:    #3c3489;  /* node-8 · structure · octagon · twilight */
--accent-lt: #6a6ab5;
```

### typography
- **Exo 2** — display, headings, kickers, labels
- **DM Sans** — body, subheads

### dark / light mode
- Default: **light mode**
- Light paper: `#f4f2ec` · card: `#fffff2` · muted: `#5a554e` · gold: `#8B5E2A` (beeswax)
- Dark paper: `#010003` · card: `#151515` · gold: `#e6c47d`
