what this is

The Quantum CEO is who we are being — not what we are doing.

It's the version of you that holds self-awareness and awareness of others simultaneously — what transpersonal psychology calls being a witness to yourself in relation to the world. Not detached. Not above it. Present, clear, and grounded in your own system.

In 2016, The Book of Shadows series began as a set of intention-setting novellas — eight chapters, eight poems each, written for the busy professional, parent, and technologist who thinks in systems and feels in metaphors. Each poem is also a song. Each story, a mirror.

They were written to teach values — not mine, but the reader's own, waiting to be remembered. The non-fiction companion, Quantum Currency: Field Research on Human Coherence, is where the inner work meets the outer world: backed by rigorous research, it takes a multi-discipline approach to the practical architecture of a coherent life that actually sustains you, the planet, and the future of tech.
